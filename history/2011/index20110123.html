Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-01-23 to 2011-01-30 (week 4)

Romans 12:9-21

  • Pray that our love would be genuine
  • Pray that we would hate that which is evil and hold on to what is good
  • Pray that we would also have brotherly affection
  • Pray that we would try to outdo one another is showing honour
  • Pray that we would be zealous and fervent in spirit
  • Pray that we will serve the Lord
  • Pray that we always rejoice in hope and be patient in tribulation
  • Pray that we would be constant in prayer
  • Pray that we contribute to the needs of the saints and seek to show hospitality
  • Bless those who persecute you
  • Rejoice with those that rejoice
  • Weep with those that weep
  • Pray that we will live in harmony with one another
  • Pray against classicism and pray that we may be associated with the lowly
  • Pray against being conceited (having an excessively favourable opinion of one's abilities, appearance, etc)
  • Pray that we would not repay evil with evil but let God repay
  • Pray that we would always do the honourable thing.
  • Pray that we would live in peace with one another.
  • Pray that we would feed our enemy if he is hungry, give him something to drink if he is thirsty
  • Pray that we would overcome evil with good
