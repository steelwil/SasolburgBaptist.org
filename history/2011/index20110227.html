The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-02-27 to 2011-03-6 (week 9)

James 5:5-10

  • Pray for those that are living on earth in luxury and for self-indulgence - that they may rather live for God and his kingdom
  • Repent of those areas in our lives where we pursue luxury and self-indulgence - that we would rather pursue things that count
  • Pray that we would not have fat hearts in the day of slaughter but that we would rather have the heart of Christ
  • Repent on behalf of our community and country for the killing of righteous people
  • Pray for those that think that nobody resists their evil, that they may turn and rather become true disciples of Christ
  • Pray that we be patient until the coming of our Lord
  • Thank God that there will be late rains, and that we be patient in waiting
  • Pray that we would be fully ready of the coming of the Lord
  • Pray that we would not grumble with one another - so that we may not be judged
  • Pray that each person in our community will know of the pending judgement that is coming (that this will lead to a change of heart)
  • Let us look to the prophets who spoke in the name of the Lord as an example of patience and suffering
