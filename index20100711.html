The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-07-12 to 2010-07-18 (week 28)

Revelation 2:4-5 and John 14:15-18

  • Let us / our community remember their God and turn back to Him.
  • Let us / our community return to the spiritual things we used to do (prayer, quite times, gathering together, being the salt/light of the world, etc.)
  • Help us be committed to our love for God and question our love for Him based on what we are doing.
  • Let us appreciate the Holy Spirit (by listening to his leading/commands)
  • Thank God that despite our failings the the Holy Spirit will be with us forever.
  • Praise God that we have the Spirit of truth in us, something the world does not know or see.
  • Thank God that we are never alone and that through the Spirit Jesus is always with us.
