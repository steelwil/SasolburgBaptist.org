The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2012-04-08 to 2012-04-15 (week 15)

Ephesians 4:25-32

  • Pray that all will be committed to putting falsehood away.
  • Pray that we would always speak the truth.
  • Thank God that we are all members of one another.
  • Pray that when we get angry that we will not sin and that we would not let the sun go down on our anger.
  • Pray that we would not give the devil any opportunity.
  • Pray that we would not longer steal but rather work honestly.
  • Pray that we would share with those in need and that we would see the need.
  • Pray that no corrupt talk will come out of our mouths.
  • Pray that we would build each other up.
  • Pray that we would not grieve the Holy Spirit.
  • Praise God that we have been sealed for the day of redemption.
  • Pray that all bitterness, wrath, anger, clamour, slander and malice be put away from us.
  • Pray that we would be kind and tender hearted and forgiving (that this be our witness).
  • Thank God that in Christ he forgave us.
