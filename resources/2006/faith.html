Faith

The trust in God and in His promises as made through Christ and the Scriptures by which humans are justified or saved.

Getting Rid of Doubt
Discovering Jesus Jesus chose to make himself known through the Church. "But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth." Acts 1:3
We can do it God gives us the power to make himself know to the world. He gives us this power through the Holy Spirit. "Again Jesus said, 'Peace be with you! As the Father has sent me, I am sending you.' " John 20:21
We can do greater miracles than Jesus We have been given great power, but are we walking in the reality of the Scriptures. Do we believe what we read in the Bible or do we only see it as "good stuff" and have doubt in our hearts. "I tell you the truth, anyone who has faith in me will do what I have been doing. He will do even greater things than these, because I am going to the Father." John 14:12
Faith, heart and the spoken word We must have faith so that what we believe what we say in our heart. We see that faith, heart (believe) and word (what we say) all need to work together. "I tell you the truth, if anyone says to this mountain, 'Go, throw yourself into the sea,' and does not doubt in his heart but believes that what he says will happen, it will be done for him." Mark 11:23
How God did it God called things into being which did not exist yet. He called into being what was in His heart. "And God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light." Genesis 1:3
The power of our words There is something about what we say that has a prefound affect on our entire body. Our tongue can determine our destiny. It's only through the grace of God that we are saved from ourselves and our words. "We all stumble in many ways. If anyone is never at fault in what he says, he is a perfect man, able to keep his whole body in check." James 3:2

"When we put bits into the mouths of horses to make them obey us, we can turn the whole animal." James 3:3"

Likewise the tongue is a small part of the body, but it makes great boasts. Consider what a great forest is set on fire by a small spark." James 3:5
False humility

If you have the Word of God in your heart and walk in faith, then you begin to believe and something takes place in your life.

It's important that we root out false humility from our lives. Sometimes we are too scared to speak out what we are. The apostle Paul was not scared and started all his letters by saying that he is an "apostle".

"Paul, an apostle of Christ Jesus by the will of God, ..." 2 Corinthians 1:1

"Paul, an apostle - sent not from men nor by man,..." Galatians 1:17
Getting rid of doubt We have to speak out the promise of God. Don't just think about it, speaking it out help us to get rid of any doubt that might be in our hearts.
