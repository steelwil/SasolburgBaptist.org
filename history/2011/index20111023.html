Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-10-23 to 2011-10-39 (week 43)

Luke 4:18-19

  • Praise Jesus for his anointing.
  • Praise the Spirit for also being with us through the sacrifice of Jesus on the cross.
  • Pray that the good news continue to be proclaimed (through us).
  • Pray that captives (sickness, poverty, crime, unemployment, addictions) will continue to be liberated.
  • Pray that the eyes of the blind (physical as well as spiritual) be opened.
  • Pray for the liberty to those who are oppressed. (demonic oppression, curses, forced situations)
  • Praise God that this is the year of his favour - Pray that we as a community will walk more and more it.
