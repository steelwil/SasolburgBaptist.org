The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2012-10-21 to 2012-10-28 (week 43)

Hebrews 12:12-17

  • Pray that the church be strengthened.
  • Pray that there will be no obstacles hampering the weak in faith.
  • Pray that the lame (those weak in faith) not fall away but rather be restored in faith.
  • Pray that there would be peace in our lives and with everybody.
  • Pray that we would strive for holiness.
  • Pray that no one will fail to receive the grace of God.
  • Pray that no "root of bitterness" springs up in our lives.
  • Pray that God would keep us on His path and that we would not make mistakes that we would regret.
  • Pray that we would look to our spiritual inheritance and never give it up.
