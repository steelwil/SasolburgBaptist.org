The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-07-19 to 2010-07-25 (week 29)

John 15:1-11

  • Let us see Jesus as the true vine and the Father as the vinedresser.
  • Let us embrace Jesus pruning us that we may bear more fruit.
  • We are clean because of the Word spoken to us, pray that our community may be rooted into the Word
  • Help us to remain is Jesus as we cannot bear fruit without him.
  • Pray that we may know the consequences of abandoning Christ as that branch will wither and thrown into the fire.
  • If we abide in Christ ask whatever we wish and it will be done for us.
  • Pray that whatever we ask will be to the Fathers glory
  • Pray that whatever we ask will be so that we may bear more fruit
  • Let us be true disciples for Christ
  • Help us to remain in Christ's love
  • Let us be obedient to Christ's command, in so doing we will remain in his love.
  • Let us have the joy of Christ in our hearts
  • Let us be full of joy.
