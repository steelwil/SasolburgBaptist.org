Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-05-24 to 2010-05-30 (week 21)

2 Peter 1:1-8

  • Thank God that our faith is obtained by the righteousness of our saviour Jesus Christ
  • Thank God that by his power we have been granted all things, through the knowledge of him
  • Pray that we can walk in our calling of glory and excellence
  • Thank God that we may be partakers of his promises and divine nature.
  • Thank God that he has given us a way to escape sin
  • Pray that we may supplement our faith with virtue (excellence), knowledge, self-control, steadfastness, godliness, brotherly affection and love.
  • Pray that we may be effective in the knowledge of Jesus Christ
