Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-03-20 to 2011-03-27 (week 12)

John 10:7-11 1 Corinthians 13

  • Pray that all would see Jesus as the only way to everlasting life
  • Pray that people would not be deceived by false teachers
  • Pray that more and more people would enter the door of Jesus and be saved.
  • Pray for protection from the thief who steals and destroys
  • Pray that in Christ we may have life and life in abundance
  • Thank Jesus for being the good shepherd
  • Thank Jesus for laying down his life for the sheep
  • Pray that we would have love and not be a clanging cymbal
  • Pray that we would embrace love because without love we are nothing
  • Pray that we would realise that without love we gain nothing
  • Pray that we would be kind, not envy or boast, not be arrogant or rude, insist on our own way
  • Pray that we would not be irritable or resentful or rejoice in wrongdoing
  • Pray that we would rejoice in truth and bear all things and continue to have hope and endurance
  • Praise God that one day we will know fully
  • Let us continue to hold on to faith, hope and love the greatest of these
