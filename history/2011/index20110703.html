The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-07-03 to 2011-07-10 (week 27)

1 Corinthians 12:12-27 Ephesians 5:25-27

  • Thank God that we are part of the body of Christ.
  • Thank God that we are all made to drink one Spirit.
  • Pray that the church would be united and that we would not think of only one member but the whole body.
  • Pray that the church would not look down on the lesser parts of the body.
  • Pray that we would accept the way God arranged to body.
  • Pray that we would realise how indispensable the weaker parts are.
  • Thank God that he has given honour to those parts that have lacked it.
  • Pray that there will be no division in the body and that we would care for one another.
  • Pray that we would see that if one parts suffers we all suffer together.
  • Thank God that if one part is honoured we can all rejoice together.
  • Thank and praise God that we are part of the body of Christ and individually member of it.
  • Thank Jesus for his love for the church and for giving himself up for her.
  • Thank Jesus for sanctifying the church and cleansing her.
  • Pray that the word would continue to wash us and the church.
  • Thank Jesus that he can present us his church to himself in splendour without sport or wrinkle.
  • Thank Jesus that he has made us his church holy.
