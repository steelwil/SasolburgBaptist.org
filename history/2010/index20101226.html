Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2010-12-26 to 2011-01-02 (week 52)

Psalm 18:31-42

  • Thank God that he is our rock - that his commitment toward us is steadfast.
  • Thank God that he gives us strength and makes our way blameless
  • Thank God that he equips us to do what we need to do for his kingdom
  • Thank God for his strength, his shield of salvation
  • Thank God for his support
  • Thank God that in him we are sure footed
  • Thank God that in him we have victory over darkness
  • Pray that we may not give up until the works of evil are utterly destroyed.
  • Thank God that the enemy is afraid and we need not be afraid
  • Praise God that in him there is victory
