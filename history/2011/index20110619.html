Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-06-19 to 2011-06-26 (week 25)

Acts 2:17-21 Luke 4:18-19

  • Pray that God's spirit be poured on us and all flesh
  • Pray that we will prophesy and see visions
  • Pray that we would dream dreams
  • Pray that even the servants will receive God's spirit and prophesy
  • Pray that God will show wonders and signs
  • Praise God that he is going to return
  • Pray that everybody will call on the name of the Lord and be saved
  • Pray that the Spirit of the Lord be upon us
  • Pray that we may receive the anointing to proclaim good news to the poor
  • Pray that we may proclaim liberty to the captives
  • Pray that we may be used to recover the sight of the blind
  • Pray that we set at liberty those who are oppressed
  • Pray that we may proclaim the year of the Lord's favour.
