Overcome the Enemy Through Christ's Blood
In a War

"And there was war in heaven: Michael and his angels going forth to war with the dragon; and the dragon warred and his angels;" Revelation 12:7

 We need to realise that we are in a war if we obey God's command and uphold the testimony of Christ.
Satan Will Appose You "because we would fain have come unto you, I Paul once and again; and Satan hindered us."

Are there doors closing in our lives because Satan is closing them? We need to be able to discern why the door closed. Is it God closing the door, or is it really the enemy.
Perseverance "Fear not the things which thou art about to suffer: behold, the devil is about to cast some of you into prison, that ye may be tried; and ye shall have tribulation ten days. Be thou faithful unto death, and I will give thee the crown of life." Revelation 2:10 The immature will give up when things get tough. The mature will rely on the power of the blood of Christ to carry them through the situation.

Overcoming Through the Blood

 You can only overcome through the finshed work of Christ. Appropriating what Christ did on the cross for us.

"And they overcame him because of the blood of the Lamb, and because of the word of their testimony; and they loved not their life even unto death." Revelation 12:7

I write unto you, young men, because ye have overcome the evil one. I have written unto you, little children, because ye know the Father. I have written unto you, fathers, because ye know him who is from the beginning. I have written unto you, young men, because ye are strong, and the word of God abideth in you, and ye have overcome the evil one." 1 John 2:12-14
