Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-07-24 to 2011-07-31 (week 30)

1 Corinthians 12:27-31

  • Pray for unity in the body of Christ
  • Pray that we may realise that we are part of something bigger
  • Pray that all in the church would recognise the hierarchy of the church of apostles, prophets, teachers, then miracles then healing, helping, administrating and tongues.
  • Pray that we would accept that we all do not have the same gifting.
  • Pray that we may have a desire to earnestly desire the higher gifts
