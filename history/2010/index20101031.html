Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2010-10-31 to 2010-11-07 (week 44)

John 17:20-26

  • Pray that all believers may be one, just as Jesus and the Father are one
  • Pray that we would be one with God
  • Pray that the world may see this unity so that the world will see that Jesus has been sent by God
  • Praise Jesus that he has given us his church his glory so that we may be one
  • Pray that we may be perfectly one - That the world may know that Jesus was sent by God
  • Pray that the world see that God loves us.
  • Pray that we may see Christ's glory - that the Father has given him
  • Praise God that we know the Father
  • Praise God that we know his name
  • Pray that God's name may continue to be made known
  • Pray/Praise that his love may be in us and that Jesus would be with us
