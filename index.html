Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2012-12-01 to 2012-01-08 (week 1)

Romans 5:6-11

  • Praise Jesus that he died for us in our sin/weakness.
  • Pray that the ungodly will see that Jesus died for them and understand the gospel.
  • Praise God for his love, that he even loved us while we were still sinners - his love for all people.
  • Thank Jesus that he died for us.
  • Praise God for blood of Jesus through which we are justified.
  • Pray that more people would know the consequences of sin (God's wrath) and that it is the blood of Christ that justifies.
  • Pray that all will know that only Jesus can reconcile us to God. (Pray against Satan's deception in this area (false religions) - that the truth may be known to all)
  • Praise God that we are saved.
  • Rejoice in God through our Lord Jesus Christ.
