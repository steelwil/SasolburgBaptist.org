Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-06-07 to 2010-06-13 (week 23)

1 John 3:18-24

  • Pray that we may love not only in word but in deed.
  • Let us listen to out hearts and let our hearts not condemn us
  • Praise God that he knows everything
  • Let us be pure before God so that we may have confidence
  • Thank God that we receive whatever we ask because ...
  • That we do what pleases him.
  • That we believe in the name of his Son Jesus Christ
  • That we would love one another
  • That we would abide in God by doing what he wants
  • Thank God that he abides is us because of the Spirit
  • Thank God that he has given us the Spirit
