The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-10-24 to 2010-10-31 (week 43)

Romans 5:1-5

  • Thank God that we have been justified by faith through Jesus Christ - that people would know the truth of this.
  • Thank God that we have peace with God through Jesus Christ - that people would know this peace
  • Pray that people would by faith enter into God's grace
  • Let us rejoice in the hope of the glory of God
  • Let us rejoice in our sufferings - pray for our siblings in Christ that are suffering
  • Let us realize that sufferings produces endurance - pray that we my have endurance
  • Pray that we may have character produced from endurance
  • Pray that we may have hope which is produced from character
  • Praise God that the hope we have is real
  • Pray that God would pour his love into our hearts through the Holy Spirit
  • Praise God that he has poured his love into our hearts through the Holy Spirit
