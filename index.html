Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2012-06-17 to 2012-06-24 (week 25)

2 Thessalonians 2:16-3:5

  • Pray that we may receive eternal comfort and hope through grace.
  • Pray for comfort in our hearts.
  • Pray that we would be established in every good work and word.
  • Pray for fellow believers.
  • Pray that the word of the Lord speed ahead and be honoured.
  • Pray that our community be delivered from wicked and evil men.
  • Pray that faith be established in more people
  • Praise God for his faithfulness
  • Thank God that he will establish us and guard us against the evil one.
  • Pray that we would have confidence that we are all doing the things of the Lord. (Unity)
  • Pray that the Lord direct our hearts to the love of God and the steadfastness of Christ.
