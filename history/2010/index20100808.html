Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-08-09 to 2010-08-16 (week 32)

Mark 16:15-20

  • Pray that the church would rise up and proclaim the gospel to the whole world.
  • Pray that all people will know that if they believe they will be saved or if they do not they will be condemned
  • Pray that the church will go out in power and know the authority we have in Christ.
  • Pray that signs would accompany the church that demons be cast out, that new tongues be spoken, that people would be healed
  • Pray for God's protection over the workers, that they will not be harmed by serpents or poison or anything else
  • Know that Jesus is at the right hand of God
  • Know that as we go out, and the Lord works with us, that the message will be confirmed with signs
