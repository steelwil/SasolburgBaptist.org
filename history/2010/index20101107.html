Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2010-11-07 to 2010-11-14 (week 45)

2 Timothy 3:10-17

  • Let us be united in the same teaching (of Paul)
  • Let us be united in our aim in life
  • Let us be united if faith
  • Let us be united if patience
  • Let us be united it love
  • Let us be united in steadfastness, persecutions and sufferings
  • Let us have the confidence that the Lord will be our rescuer
  • Let us expect persecutions if we want to live a godly life
  • Let us not be disheartened by evil - which would get worse
  • Pray that we may continue in what we have learned and firmly believe
  • Pray that we may experience the wisdom of the sacred writings
  • Pray that we may be wise for salvations through faith in Jesus
  • Praise God that all scripture is from him and is good for teaching, reproof and correction and training in righteousness
  • Praise God that we are equipped and competent for every good work
