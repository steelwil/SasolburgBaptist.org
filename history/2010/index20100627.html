The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-06-28 to 2010-07-04 (week 26)

Jude 1:17-23

  • Remember the prophetic words of the apostles concerning Jesus.
  • Help us to recognize and not fall for the arguments of those that mock Christianity
  • Help us to follow the Spirit and not cause devision
  • Pray that we may be built up in faith - meditate on what faith is
  • Pray in the Spirit - let us be in sync with the Spirit as he leads us in prayer
  • Let us have a desire and make it our work to love God
  • Thank Jesus for his mercy and that through him we have eternal life
  • Let us have mercy for those who doubt and not give up on them.
  • Pray that God will show us how we can snatch the lost out of the fire
  • Help us to show mercy with fear - meditate on Gods mercy and how we can do likewise
  • Help us to hate the works of the flesh (verses the Spirit)
