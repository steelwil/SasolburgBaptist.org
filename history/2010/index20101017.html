The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-10-17 to 2010-10-24 (week 42)

Colossians 2:1-5

  • Let us have a heart for other communities even though we may not have seen them face to face.
  • Pray that our/their hearts may be encouraged (how can we be used in this area)
  • Pray that we may be knit together in love. (that we may be taught by Jesus to love)
  • Pray that we may reach all the riches of understanding and knowledge of God's mystery
  • Pray that we would realize that it is through Jesus this can be obtained (Jesus is the way, the truth and the life - John 14:6)
  • Pray that we would receive treasures of wisdom and knowledge through Christ
  • Let us not listen to plausible arguments (listen rather to the Spirit - Revelation 3:22)
  • Let us rejoice/pray that we remain firm in faith.
