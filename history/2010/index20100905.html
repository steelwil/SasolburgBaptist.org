Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-09-05 to 2010-09-12 (week 36)

2 Corinthians 4:1-6

  • Let us not lose heart but persevere in what we are doing for the Lord
  • Let us/those in our community renounce disgraceful ways
  • Let us/those in our community not try to twist Gods word
  • Let us listen to our conscience, and that the veil be lifted from those that are perishing
  • Let us pray against the deceptions of the evil one so that the lost can see the light
  • Let the glory of Christ be known in our community/lives
  • Let us be the voice of God and not ourselves when we proclaim Jesus as Lord
  • Let us be servants for Jesus
  • So that we may be light in the darkness
  • Pray that this light shine on all peoples that they would come to the knowledge of the glory of God
