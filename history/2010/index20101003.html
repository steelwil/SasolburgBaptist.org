The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-10-03 to 2010-10-10 (week 40)

Galatians 5:1
Ephesians 3:17
Romans 8:31-39

  • Praise Christ that he has set us free (that our community would walk in this)
  • Pray that we would always walk in this freedom no matter what the circumstance
  • Pray that we would not slip into the yoke of slavery (distress, persecution, fear, tribulation, danger ...)
  • Pray that Christ may dwell in our hearts through faith (Praise him that he does dwell ...)
  • Pray that we may be rooted and grounded in love
  • Thank God that he is for us and because of this we need not fear those who are against us
  • Praise God for not sparing his own son for our sakes
  • Praise God for graciously giving us all things
  • Praise God that we are justified, no longer condemned.
  • Praise God that Jesus was raised and intercedes for us at the right hand of God
  • Praise God that even though we may be regarded as sheep to the slaughter it will not separate us from God's love
  • Praise God that we are more than conquerors through him who loved us
  • Praise God that nothing (death, life, angles, rulers, powers) can separate us from the love of God in Jesus
  • Praise him that he is OUR Lord
  • let us hold on to hope
  • and be bold
  • pray that our minds might not be hardened
  • pray that people may turn to the Lord that the veil may be lifted
  • Let us all experience the freedom in the Spirit
  • Let those with unveiled faces behold the Glory of the Lord
  • Be still and know that he is God (Psalms 46:10)
  • Let us draw near to God knowing that he will draw near to you (James 4:8)
  • Pray that we may be transformed into the image of this glory (God)
  • Thank God and praise him for what he wants/has given to us
