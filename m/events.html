Faith Mountain

An interdenominational intercessory prayer meeting focusing on the restoration of our region.

WHO ARE Prayer Mountain Intercessors?
They are a willing group of people from the Vaal triangle, who come together only once a month to bow the knee before God and pray for the community. They know that no preaching, teaching church can bring a peaceful and prosperous community. Their heart in not just for their own church, but is the heart of God, who cares for the whole community. Their vision is beyond the now of time and seasons but their eyes are set on the Kingdom of God.
