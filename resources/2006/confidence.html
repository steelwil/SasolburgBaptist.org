A Place of Confidence, A Place of Rest
Faith, More Precious Than Gold

"That the proof of your faith, being more precious than gold that perishith though it is proved by fire, may be found unto praise and glory and honour at the revelation of Jesus Christ:"   1 Peter 1:7

 With gold you can buy a lot, but the faith that God has given us is worth much more.
To "Know" Him

"These things I have written unto you that believe on the name of the Son of God; that ye may know that ye have eternal life, and that ye may believe on the name of the Son of God" 1 John 5:13

"And hereby we do know that we know him, if we keep his commandments.  He that saith, I know him, and keepeth not his commandments, is a liar, and the truth is not in him. But whoso keepeth his word, in him verily is the love of God perfected: hereby know we that we are in him. He that saith he abideth in him ought himself also so to walk, even as he walked." 1 John 2:2-6

Satan deceives people by letting them think that they are saved eventhough they are not obaying God and they don't love God.
Salvation is a Doorway

In the Scriptures John don't only want the church to know that they are SAVED, he also wants them to KNOW GOD. Salvation is only a doorway in the relationship with God.

 "By this we know that we love the children of God, when we love God, and keep his commandments. For this is the love of God, that we keep his commandments: and his commandments are not grievous. For whatsoever is born of God overcometh the world: and this is the victory that overcometh the world, even our faith." 1 John 2:2-4

Tossed Like the Waves

"If any of you lack wisdom, let him ask of God, that giveth to all men liberally, and upbraideth not; and it shall be given him. But let him ask in faith, nothing wavering. For he that wavereth is like a wave of the sea driven with the wind and tossed. For let not that man think that he shall receive any thing of the Lord. A double minded man is unstable in all his ways." James 1:5-8

Our confidence should not be based on our ability or what we see. Our confidence should be based on God's confidence and what He teaches us in the Scriptures.

The reason why we don't know the will of God is because we don't seek it. We need to spend time in His Word to seek His will.

A Place of Confidence, A Place of Rest

"And this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us:  And if we know that he hear us, whatsoever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we desired of him." 1 John 5:14,15

Very often we come to the Father with so much doubt in our hearts. We need to be in a place of confidence when we approach God. We need to be so assured that when we speak to God, He will answer us.
