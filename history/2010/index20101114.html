The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. Prayer Guide for 2010-11-14 to 2010-11-21 (week 46)

Luke 6:46-49

  • pray that we be united, in doing what our Lord tells us to do
  • pray that our ears may be opened and that our minds understand to hear God's word and do.
  • pray that we will not be deceived by the evil one to compromise God's word to suite our own experience or comfort (Matthew 15:6)
  • Pray that our "house" will be built on the "Rock" - that our energy will be invested in doing the word of Jesus, because then our investment will stand
  • Pray that when trouble/persecution/we are attacked (a flood) that we would remain firm and know that all floods come to an end - that in Christ we will stand
  • Pray that we will not ignore Jesus' teachings and live with false security for when the flood comes our "house" will fall - pray that the Holy Spirit will expose these areas in our lives (worry, anger, complaining ...) - that we can stop wasting our energy on things that will not stand and are not important
