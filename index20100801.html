Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-08-02 to 2010-08-09 (week 31)

Romans 3:10-26

  • pray that all may be convicted and be aware of their sin
  • pray that the lost become aware of their doom
  • pray that the Holy Spirit show the lost their ruin and misery
  • pray that their hearts be softened and that the fear of God return
  • pray that all may know that it is not by works that they will be justified
  • pray that knowledge of sin be revealed through the law
  • pray that all may know and receive righteousness through Jesus by believing
  • pray that the stronghold or righteousness be broken as all fall short of the glory of God
  • pray that all may see that we are justified by grace which is a gift in Christ
  • pray that all may receive Jesus through faith
  • Thank God for His gift of eternal life and forgiveness of our sins in Jesus Christ
