Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-07-31 to 2011-08-07 (week 31)

1 Corinthians 14:1-5

  • Pray that in whatever (gifting) we do that we would pursue it in love
  • Pray that we would earnestly desire the spiritual gifts, especially prophesy
  • Pray that we would speak to God through tongues that mysteries of the Spirit to be uttered.
  • Pray that we would use tongues to build ourselves up
  • Pray that when we prophesy that it would be for building up the church.
  • Pray that all would speak in tongues but even more to prophesy.
  • Pray that we would recognise that the one who prophesies is greater that the one who speaks on tongues
  • Pray for the gift of interpretation so that the church may be build up
