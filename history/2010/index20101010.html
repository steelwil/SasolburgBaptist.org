The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-10-10 to 2010-10-17 (week 41)

1 Thessalonians 5: 1- 11

  • Let us/Our community not be ignorant about future events (for example heaven, hell, wages of sin)
  • Let us who know not be caught unawares (but live each day fully for the Lord)
  • Pray that our community will know of the finality of the end time (that there are no second chances)
  • Pray that we would rather live each day in the light so that we would not be surprised
  • Praise God for Jesus through whom we have/can become children of the light - that this truth be preached to all nations
  • Pray that we would not (spiritually) sleep and always be sober (minded), those that sleep and are drunk are part of darkness
  • Praise God that we belong to the day
  • Pray that we would be sober and put on the breastplate of faith and love and the helmet of hope for salvation.
  • Thank God that he has destined mankind for salvation through Jesus and not destruction.
  • Praise God that we are going to live with him
  • Help us to encourage one another and build one another up - meditate on doing this through Christ's love
