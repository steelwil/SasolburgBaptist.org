Attaining a Pure Heart
The Pure in Heart "Blessed are the pure in heart: for they shall see God." Matthew 5:8 What a powerful promise that Jesus is making. This verse is not only referring to a future reality when we get to heaven, but also to the current reality because the Spirit of God is a comforter in the present as well.
The Standard "This then is the message which we have heard of him, and declare unto you, that God is light, and in him is no darkness at all." 1 John 1:5 The standard of purity is God Himself. Therefore in God's dealings with us is with a absolutely pure intention.

Imperative to Change

 "If we say that we have fellowship with him, and walk in darkness, we lie, and do not the truth:" 1 John 1:6 We cannot say that we are children in God if we live in darkness. We cannot continue with the same sin if we claim to be children of God.
Two Ways There are two reasons why we decide to change our sinful ways: out of fear or out of love. We can decide to clean up our lives out of fear that God will punish us or we can do because we love God so much that we want Him to dwell in our temple and feel welcome in our hearts.
Purification "If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness." 1 John 1:9 We have to confess our sins. It need to be an active part of our will. Many of us wish for our hearts to be clean, but we have to make the decision to confess and recieve the power of God. This is how God purify us!!

Preparation Time

 God is preparing His church to have a pure heart so that the body of Christ would be solid in Jesus when the hearvest comes. He's raising up maturity in the Body of Christ.
