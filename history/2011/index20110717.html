Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-07-17 to 2011-07-24 (week 29)

Ephesians 4:1-7

  • Pray that we may walk in a manner worthy of our calling.
  • Pray that we my also be sure of what our calling is.
  • Pray that we may be humble and gentile and be patient bearing with one another in love
  • Pray for an eagerness to maintain the unity of Spirit in the bond of peace
  • Pray that all would realise that there is one body and one Spirit and that we are called to one hope
  • Pray that we may see that we serve one Lord one faith and that there is one baptism
  • Praise our Father that he is god over all, through all and in all.
  • Praise that grace have been given to each one of us according the the measure of Christ's gift
