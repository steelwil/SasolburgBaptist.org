Covenant - 1

(download sermon)

A solemn agreement that is binding to all parties.


Two Is Better Than One
Founded on Love

When a covenant is made it is founded on love.

 "For this reason a man will leave his father and mother and be united to his wife, and they will become one flesh." Genesis 2:24
Two are Better Than One

"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their work." Ecclesiastes 4:9

This is the blessing of being a partner in marriage. To complement or complete your spouse. Making up for each other's inadequacies.
Not a Horizontal Agreement

Man has his own opinion about marriage and very often sees it as an "horizontal" agreement. In other words, an agreement between two parties on earth. But a Christian marriage is more than just a horizontal agreement. It is a powerful mystery of Christ in us and us in Christ. This is why there's such an attack on Christian marriages.

  

The Third Strand

 "Also, if two lie down together, they will keep warm. But how can one keep warm alone? Though one may be overpowered, two can defend themselves. A cord of three strands is not quickly broken" Ecclesiastes 4:11-12

There is a lot of opposition to relationships, partnerships and marriages. But Ecclesiastes 4:12 tells us that there need to be a "third strand" in order for the relationship to survive. God must be the third strand and this forms the "vertical" part of the covenant.

Why do we need God to be the third strand? Because when things get tough, we need the power of God to keep us together.
God Was There When we make marriage vows, we make it in the presence of man and God. In other words, God seals the covenant. For this reason we should call on the "third strand" when we go through difficult times in our marriage.
