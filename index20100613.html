The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-06-14 to 2010-06-20 (week 24)

1 John 4:7-12

  • Let us love one another
  • Let us become like God in his love.
  • Thank God that when we became his children he gave us the ability to love
  • Thank God for his love
  • Thank God for manifesting his love through Jesus
  • Thank Jesus that we may have life through him.
  • Thank God that he loved us first, by sending Jesus to pay for our sins
  • If God loves us we should follow his example and love others
  • We cannot see God but in love God is with us
  • That Gods love will be perfected in us.
