The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-05-29 to 2011-06-05 (week 22)

Ephesians 5:1
1 John 2:15-18

  • Pray that we would imitate our father as his beloved child.
  • Pray that we would walk in love as Christ.
  • Pray that we would also give ourselves up as a fragrant offering and sacrifice to God.
  • Pray that we would not love the world but that our love would be for our Father.
  • Pray that God would reveal those worldly areas in our lives that we love.
  • Pray that we may see that all that is in the world - the desires of the flesh and eyes and pride in possessions are not from the Father but of the world.
  • Pray that we may realise that the world is temporary and passing away.
  • Pray that we may do the will of God so that we may abide forever
  • Pray that we may realise that time is short as this is the last hour.
