The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-03-13 to 2011-03-20 (week 11)

1 Thessalonians 5:16-22 Jeremiah 29:11-14

  • Pray that we as a community would rejoice always (and not grumble or complain)
  • Pray that we would be in constant communication with God through prayer
  • Pray that we would be thankful in all circumstances - for this is God's will.
  • Pray that we would not quench the spirit (by looking at circumstances) but look at God's plan (for your life)
  • Let us rather hold on the the prophecies and what God's word says about the matter
  • Pray that we would always test everything and hold on to that which is good
  • Pray against the work and fruit of the evil one by abstaining from all forms of evil
  • Pray that we would focus on God's plan for our community and our lives
  • Praise God that his plans for us are plans for our welfare and not for evil to give us a future and a hope
  • Thank God that when we call upon the Lord and pray to him, he will hear us
  • Pray that more and more people will seek God will all their heart
  • Praise God that we will find Him
