Faith, the Key to Joy
Joy in Trials "My brethren, count it all joy when ye fall into divers temptations" James 1:2 How can we be full of joy when we are facing life's trials? This is a tough ask.
Where to Focus We get despondent when we're in a trial because we fix our eyes on the situation. Looking at the situation inspires fear within us. "Looking unto Jesus the author and finisher of our faith; who for the joy that was set before him endured the cross, despising the shame, and is set down at the right hand of the throne of God." Hebrews 12:2
Trials Makes Us Stronger The trails we're facing are in actual fact a gift from God. It's there to help us to depend more on Him and our faith is increased every time He comes through for us. "And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose." Romans 8:28
Faith and Sin Not to have faith is to sin. If we have doubt and unbelief in our hearts we're sinning against God. "And he that doubteth is damned if he eat, because he eateth not of faith: for whatsoever is not of faith is sin." Romans 14:23
Joy Through Faith "So then faith cometh by hearing, and hearing by the word of God." Romans 10:17

The number one reason we loose our joy is because we're putting down our shield of faith. Faith is a confident expectation of a future outcome.

Faith and joy are interlinked because where there's faith, there's hope, and where there's hope, there's joy.
