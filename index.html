Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2013-03-17 to 2013-03-24 (week 12)

Luke 2:47-49 ESV John 13:34-35 ESV

  • Worship Jesus for His amazing understanding and answers (to our life's problems)
  • Pray that we would be about the Fathers business.
  • Pray we as believers would love one another, as Jesus has loved us.
  • Pray that all people will know that we are the disciples of Jesus, through the love we have for one another. That this be our witness.
