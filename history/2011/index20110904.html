Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-08-28 to 2011-09-04 (week 35)

Romans 12:3-8

  • Praise God for the grace given to us
  • Pray that we would not think more highly of ourselves than we ought.
  • Pray for sober judgement
  • Thank God for the measure of faith that he has assigned us.
  • Pray that every person would know their function in the body.
  • Pray that we would embrace the different gifts as we are members of one another.
  • Pray that all would use their gifts according to the grace given to them.
  • Pray for prophecy, serving, teaching, exhortation, giving, leadership and mercy to be done accordingly.
