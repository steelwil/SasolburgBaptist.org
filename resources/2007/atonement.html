Atonement

To be made one with God. Satisfaction or a reparation for a wrong or injury.

Made One with God
Why Christ Died

"For if, when we were enemies, we were reconciled to God by the death of his Son, much more, being reconciled, we shall be saved by his life. And not only so, but we also joy in God through our Lord Jesus Christ, by whom we have now received the atonement." Romans 5:10-11

 God provides a way in which man can come to have intimacy and be reconciled with Him.

Four Vital Elements

 What are the different components concerning atonement?

With atonement, there is firstly an offense that needs to be removed. Secondly, the person against whom the offense has been committed needs to be pacified. Thirdly, there is the guilty person that have committed the offense. And fourthly, a sacrifice needs to be made in order to bring reconciliation. John Owen

Why Do We Need Atonement

"For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven against all ungodliness and unrighteousness of men, who hold the truth in unrighteousness;"  Romans 1:18

 

"Much more then, being now justified by his blood, we shall be saved from wrath through him." Romans 5:9

 Sin provokes God's anger. We are sinners and God is in opposition to this sin in us. This is why we cannot be one with God, hence the reconciliation, the atonement.

Restored

 When we come to the place of repentance we will be restored, just like the prodigal son. Through Jesus we have been reconciled to the Father.
