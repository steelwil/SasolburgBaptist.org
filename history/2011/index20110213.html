Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-01-13 to 2011-02-20 (week 7)

John 14:1-14

  • Pray that our hearts will not be troubled.
  • Pray that we would believe in God
  • Thank Jesus that he is preparing a room on the Fathers house for us
  • Thank Jesus that he is going to take us to himself so that we can be with him
  • Pray that all will understand the truth of the gospel and not be confused
  • Pray that all will see Jesus as the way the truth and the life
  • Acknowledge that no one comes to the Father except through Jesus
  • Thank Jesus that that if we know him we also know the Father
  • Thank Jesus that through him we know the Father
  • Pray that with time we would grow with a greater revelation of who the Father is.
  • Praise Jesus who has the Father in him
  • Pray that we would also be vessels to do the Fathers work
  • Pray that we would produce fruit showing that God is in us.
  • Praise God that Jesus is with the Father and because of this we can do even greater works that what Jesus did.
  • Praise God that whatever we ask in Jesus name Jesus will do
  • Pray that the Father may be glorified in the Son
  • Thank Jesus that whatever we ask in his name he will do
