Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-01-09 to 2011-01-16 (week 2)

Colossians 1:15-20 Matthew 6:25-33

  • Praise Jesus as the firstborn of creation
  • Praise Jesus through which all things were created
  • Praise Jesus the head of the church
  • Praise Jesus that through his blood we are able to be reconciled to him
  • Pray that we may not be anxious about life's necessities
  • Praise God that he cares for us
  • Help us to have faith
  • Pray that we may first seek (pursue) his kingdom
  • Thank God that he will take care of our needs if we seek first his kingdom
