The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2011-05-29 to 2011-06-05 (week 22)

Luke 9:57-62

  • Pray that more people will get the desire to sincerely follow Jesus wherever he wants us to go.
  • Pray that we would count the cost of following Jesus accepting it and persevering when there is no place to lay one's head.
  • Pray that we may follow Jesus whole heartedly without any conditions or personal limitations.
  • Pray that we may heed to to call to proclaim the kingdom of God when called.
  • Pray that we would love following Jesus more than anything else (family).
  • Pray that we may persevere once we have put our had to the plough and never look back.
