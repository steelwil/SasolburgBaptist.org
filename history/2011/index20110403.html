Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-03-27 to 2011-04-03 (week 13)

Matthew 18:16-20 Mark 4:35-41

  • Pray that we as a community would always follow the direction of Jesus
  • Worship Jesus and pray that we would not doubt in any way
  • Praise Jesus that all authority is given to him in heaven and on earth
  • Pray that we would go out and make disciples of all nations baptising them in the name of the Father, Son and Holy Spirit
  • Pray that all would be taught the ways of Jesus
  • Thank Jesus that he is with us always to the end of the age.
  • Pray that even in the storm we would not be afraid but rather have faith.
