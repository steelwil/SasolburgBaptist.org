Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Prayer Guide for 2010-05-31 to 2010-06-06 (week 22)

1 Peter 4:7-11

  • Help us to be sober-minded and self-controlled for the sake of our prayers
  • Help us to continue to love one another earnestly
  • Thank God for his love in the cross which covers a multitude of sins
  • Help us to show hospitality to one another without grumbling
  • Thank God for the gift we have received
  • Help us to use our gift to serve one another
  • Pray that whatever we do that we do it in the strength God supplies
  • That our serving be to the glory of God through Jesus Christ.
  • Worship God for he has glory and dominion forever
