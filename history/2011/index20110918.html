Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2011-09-11 to 2011-09-18 (week 37)

Hebrews 9:11-14 1 Corinthians 10:14-17

  • Praise Jesus that he is our high priest
  • Praise Jesus for his blood that secures us eternal redemption.
  • Praise Jesus that through his blood we are purified so that we can server the living God
  • Pray that our community would flee from idolatry
  • Pray for people to be sensible
  • Pray that we would understand the cup, which is the participation in the blood of Christ.
  • Pray that we would understand that the bread we break, is participation in the body of Christ.
  • Pray that we would understand that we are part of one body all partaking of the one bread.
