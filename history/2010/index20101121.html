Home Page

The Sasolburg Baptist Church is a member of the Baptist Union of Southern Africa. The Church acknowledges Jesus Christ as the Supreme Head of the Church, and undertakes to manage all its affairs according to the New Testament teaching and recognises the sole authority of the Holy Scriptures in all matters of faith and conduct.

Vision: "Revealing the heart of God to all nations"

Prayer Guide for 2010-11-21 to 2010-11-28 (week 47)

1 Timothy 1:18-2:7

  • pray that we would follow the prophecies made about us
  • pray that we would wage the good warfare of faith
  • pray that we may hold on to faith and good conscience
  • pray that we may not shipwreck our faith - pray also for those who have fallen back
  • pray that we may we may continue with prayers of requests, intercessions and thanksgivings for those in high positions
  • pray that we may continue in this so that we may live in peace, be godly and dignified in every way
  • pray that in this we may please God our Saviour
  • thank God that he desires all people to be saved and that we would share his heart in this regard
  • pray that we and our leaders may come to the knowledge of the truth
  • pray that all will come to the knowledge that there is one God and one mediator between God and man
  • pray that people will come to the knowledge of the truth, that Jesus is the ransom for our sins - that they may receive the gospel
  • pray that we would follow our calling and teach one another in faith and truth
