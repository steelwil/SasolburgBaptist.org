Name

download sermon

A word by which an entity is designated and distinguished from others.

Let His Name be Lifted High
The Value of a Name What is the most valuable natural asset that you have? Our desire should be to have a good name. "A good name is rather to be chosen than great riches, and loving favour rather than silver and gold." Proverbs 22:1
Disillusioned by His Name What reputation does the name of Jesus have? Does the believer trust in the name of Jesus or is he feeling disillusioned by it because Jesus did not come through for him?  
God's Promise "And Moses went up unto God, and the LORD called unto him out of the mountain, saying, Thus shalt thou say to the house of Jacob, and tell the children of Israel; Ye have seen what I did unto the Egyptians, and how I bare you on eagles' wings, and brought you unto myself." Exodus 19:3-4 The name of God was held in high esteem by the people of Israel because God made a promise to them upon which He delivered.

Forgiven for His Name's Sake

 "But they rebelled against me, and would not hearken unto me: they did not every man cast away the abominations of their eyes, neither did they forsake the idols of Egypt: then I said, I will pour out my fury upon them, to accomplish my anger against them in the midst of the land of Egypt. But I wrought for my name' sake, that it should not be polluted before the heathen, among whom they were , in whose sight I made myself known unto them, in bringing them forth out of the land of Egypt." Ezekiel 20:8-9

God often have mercy on us for His name's sake.
Defending God's Name "O sing unto the LORD a new song: sing unto the LORD, all the earth. Sing unto the LORD, bless his name; shew forth his salvation from day to day. Declare his glory among the heathen, his wonders among all people." Psalms 96:1-3 What are we declaring to people about God's name. God's name is at stake.

 Satan's Plan

 "He had been hired to intimidate me so that I would commit a sin by doing this, and then they would give me a bad name to discredit me." Nehemiah 6:13

Satan will do whatever he can to distract us from our devotion to discredit the name of Jesus. He knows the power in the name of Jesus. He's hoping that when the world looks at us in our failure that they would say they don't want anything to do with Jesus.
God's Reputation is at Stake "Now therefore, if ye will obey my voice indeed, and keep my covenant, then ye shall be a peculiar treasure unto me above all people: for all the earth is mine: And ye shall be unto me a kingdom of priests, and an holy nation. These are the words which thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel." Exodus 19:5-6 From early on the purpose of Israel was to declare the glory of God the all the nations. The other nations' perception about God's reputation were going to be based on how God dealt with Israel and how Israel dealt with God.

God invested His name with His people.
